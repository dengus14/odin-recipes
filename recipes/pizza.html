Pizza Recipe

Description

This grilled pizza is topped with tomato sauce,
tomatoes, black olives, roasted red peppers,
mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil. Of course,
you can use whatever toppings you like!

Ingredients:

  • Pizza Dough
  • Garlic Oil
  • Pizza Toppings:
    • cup tomato sauce, divided
    • 1 cup of tomatoes
    • cups of shredded mozzarella cheese, divided

Steps:

  1. Gatler all Ingridients
  2. Make Dough
  3. Mix in flour
  4. Bake for 8 minutes
  5. Place dough in bowl
  6. Leave it for an hour
  7. Make Garlic
  8. Preheat oven
